PM SURYA GHAR SCHEME

Government Subsidy Center

1. Eligibility Check

Open to Indian resident households with an active electricity consumer connection and concrete, shadow-free roof layout area.

2. Scheme Benefits

Claim direct subsidies up to ₹78,000 for residential setups up to 3kW. Benefit from net metering by feeding back excess power.

3. Required Documentation

Latest utility bills, identification card (PAN/Aadhaar), structural roof certificate, and a verified bank account.
